# RFC-0000: RFC Template

> **Status:** Template
> Copy this file to `NNNN-short-title.md` and fill in each section.

## Summary

One paragraph explaining the change or proposal.

## Motivation

Why are we doing this? What problem does it solve? What use cases does it support?

## Guide-Level Explanation

Explain the proposal as if teaching it to another PlayOS contributor. Introduce
new concepts, terminology, and examples.

## Reference-Level Explanation

The technical detail of the proposal. Describe the design precisely enough that
an implementer could build it and a reviewer could evaluate it against the
specification.

## Drawbacks

Why should we *not* do this?

## Rationale and Alternatives

- Why is this design the best among the alternatives considered?
- What other designs were considered?
- What is the impact of not doing this?

## Prior Art

Discuss prior art, both the good and the bad, in relation to this proposal.

## Unresolved Questions

- What parts of the design are still to be determined?

## Future Possibilities

Think about what the natural extension and evolution of this proposal would be.
